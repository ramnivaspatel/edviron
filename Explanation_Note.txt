EDVIRON Revenue Analytics – Explanation Note

Revenue Logic:

The dataset consists of three pricing layers:
1. Edviron Buying Price
2. Partner Pricing
3. Merchant Pricing

Flat pricing is treated as fixed INR per transaction.
Percentage pricing is applied on Transaction Amount.

ERP Revenue = Merchant Pricing – Partner Pricing  
Edviron Net Revenue = Partner Pricing – Edviron Buying  
Edviron Gross Revenue = ERP Revenue + Edviron Net Revenue  

Settlement Assumptions:

- ERP payable only for SUCCESS transactions.
- Non-success transactions treated as exposure.
- Gateway fees inferred using Vendor Amount when applicable.
- Missing pricing assumed zero but flagged.

Dashboard Rationale:

The dashboard provides executive-level visibility into revenue performance,
partner performance, and exposure risk using dynamic slicers and pivot-based visuals.
